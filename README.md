**WishFlex** is a modern, lightweight, and **"What You See Is What You Get" (WYSIWYG)** cooldown, buff, and class resource monitor for World of Warcraft. Say goodbye to complex codes and endless configurations. WishFlex is designed to work beautifully right out of the box, helping you keep track of your most important abilities with pixel-perfect precision.

**WishFlex** 是一款现代、轻量且**“可见即所得”**的技能冷却、增益以及职业资源监控插件。
告别复杂的代码编写和繁琐的字符串导入！WishFlex 拥有极简的设置面板，开箱即用，让你用最直观的方式打造属于自己的战斗监控。

***

## ✨ Core Features
## 核心特性

### 🖱️ WYSIWYG & Edit Mode Integration
### 可见即所得 & 无缝集成

* **Zero Code Required:** No need to learn complex WeakAuras logic. Simply open the options panel, check what you want to see, and you are done.
**零代码门槛**：告别繁杂的条件触发器，只需在设置面板勾选需要的技能，即可直观预览。

* **Native Edit Mode:** Fully integrated with Blizzard's Edit Mode. Drag, drop, and resize your monitor groups directly on your screen.
**原生编辑模式**：完美嵌入暴雪系统“编辑模式”。你可以像拖拽原生动作条一样，直接在屏幕上自由拖拽、移动各个监控组。

* **Smart Magnetic Snapping:** Groups (like Utility and Buffs) can seamlessly snap to your Essential skills or player frame for a clean, aligned look.
**智能磁吸贴合**：效能技能、增益图标可以自动吸附到重要技能条或玩家头像附近，轻松对齐强迫症福音。

### 🧠 Smart Auto-Detection
### 智能识别追踪

* **Trinkets & Racials:** Automatically detects and tracks your equipped active trinkets (Slot 13/14), potions, and racial abilities. Swap your gear, and the monitor updates instantly!
**饰品与种族技能**：自动识别你当前装备的主动饰品（13/14槽）、药水和种族天赋技能。换装自动更新，无需手动输入 ID！

### 🗂️ Categorized Cooldowns
### 分类清晰的冷却监控

* **Separated Groups:** Independently track your **Essential** (core rotation), **Utility** (interrupts/movement), and **Defensive** cooldowns.
**精细分类**：将你的技能分为**重要技能**（核心输出/治疗）、**效能技能**（打断/位移）和**防御技能**（减伤），各司其职，战斗信息一目了然。

* **Custom Monitors:** Add any specific spell, item, or buff you want to keep an eye on with the Extra Monitor feature.
**自定义额外监控**：支持手动输入任意物品或法术 ID，单独加入额外监控组，绝不漏掉任何关键触发。

### 🎨 Pixel-Perfect Aesthetics
### 像素级精美视觉

* Clean borders, crisp cooldown texts, and smooth swipe animations.
高清干净的边框、锐利的倒计时字体以及丝滑的冷却动画。

* Supports Custom Overlay Glows for important procs.
支持自定义技能高亮边框（Glow），关键触发瞬间吸睛。

## ⚙️ Quick Start
## 快速开始

1. Type `/wf` or `/wishflex` in chat to open the intuitive options panel.
在聊天框输入 `/wf` 或 `/wishflex` 打开极简设置面板。

2. Check the spells or items you want to monitor.
勾选或添加你想要监控的技能与物品。

3. Open **Edit Mode (编辑模式)** via the game menu to position the frames anywhere you like.
打开游戏主菜单的 **编辑模式**，将监控组拖放到屏幕上你最舒服的位置即可！
